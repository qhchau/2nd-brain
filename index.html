⚠ INGEST TIPS
  • Rule of thumb: If you're not willing to read a document from start to finish, it's probably not worth ingesting everything. For large materials, only ingest the parts relevant to your workflow.
  • You can instruct the AI to ingest only the sections relevant to your job function.
  • The more you ingest blindly, the longer it takes the AI to locate information — which increases the chance of hallucination.
  • For large files such as engineering templates, gate reviews, or Gantt charts, consider ingesting the same file multiple times — each pass focused on a different entity type: project  |  person  |  client  |  vendor  |  concept  |  decision  |  tool  |  event
  • After a large ingest, run a lint to ensure all newly created pages are properly cross-linked to related projects, people, and decisions in the wiki.
LINT COMMAND EXAMPLE
lint the wiki to clean up
